🚀 Step 1: The Accidental SEO Trick

One of the easiest SEO hacks that worked (and still works) is meta tags optimization. I stuffed my website's head with 200+ keywords, making it look like this:

<meta name="keywords" content="Dwip Biswas, dwipbiswas, SEO hacking, Google ranking, Bing ranking, 
how to rank on Google, website SEO, backlinks, search engine optimization, Python, AI, growth hacking">

Google and Bing crawlers were like: "👀 Hold up… this site must be important!" and started ranking my site higher.

🔥 Step 2: The Backlink Madness

Then I built a Python backlink bot that spammed my site URL to every free SEO backlink submission site.

Here's the bot I used (don't try this at home 💀):

import requests

# List of backlink sites
BACKLINK_SITES = [
    "https://www.pingmylinks.com/?url=https://dwip-biswas.github.io/",
    "https://www.seoheap.com/backlink-generator/?url=https://dwip-biswas.github.io/",
    "https://www.freewebsubmission.com/?url=https://dwip-biswas.github.io/"
]

# Spam the links
for site in BACKLINK_SITES:
    response = requests.get(site)
    print(f"Submitted to {site} → Status: {response.status_code}")

After running this, hundreds of backlinks were pointing to my site, and Google ranked me even higher!
